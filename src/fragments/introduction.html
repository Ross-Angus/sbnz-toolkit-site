Introduction

Research will play a key role in helping us meet the challenges of climate change and the unpredictable economic, societal, and environmental shifts that will come with it. As a result, research funders are not only asking researchers to adopt sustainable research practice. Funders are also encouraging researchers to use multiple different approaches on climate change and sustainability, inspired by diverse perspectives1.

1 Where this toolkit is focused on the substance of research, there are other resources which help research institutions to adopt sustainable procurement and research practices in line with the Welcome Concordat for the Environmental Sustainability of Research and Innovation Practice
