Additional Resources

The Glasgow Centre for Sustainable Solutions has multiple 4-week MOOCs which can help you learn more about sustainability.

It’s also helpful to realise that you can contribute to sustainability, even if it is not the core focus of your research. Some researchers operationalise a multi-dimensional approach (‘strong sustainability’) whilst others may only engage with one dimension (‘weak sustainability’).
